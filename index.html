This is her story

PERUERE

Peruere(Arlecchino), is an orphan from the Ancient Crimson Moon Dynasty who became the "Knave" of the Fatui and led the House of the Hearth. After surviving the cruel experiments of her "mother," Crucabena, who inherited the throne of the House of the Hearth, she killed Crucabena and transformed the House of the Hearth from a cruel "mother" to a strict but caring "father," giving the children in her care the freedom to choose their own futures.

Crimson Moon Semblance

Grants a Bond of Life equal to 18% of Max HP when a Charged Attack hits an opponent. This effect can be triggered up to once every 14s. In addition, when the equipping character has a Bond of Life, they gain a 12% DMG Bonus; if the value of the Bond of Life is greater than or equal to 20% of Max HP, then gain an additional 24% DMG Bonus.
